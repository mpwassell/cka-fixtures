CKA League Fixtures Change and Postpone Requests

Use this page if you are the home club for a fixture and you want to do one of the following:
  • You want to indicate that the fixture needs to be changed. Click 'Pending' against the fixture in the list below.
  • You want to indicate that the change to the fixture has been agreed. Click 'Agreed' against the fixture in the list below.
Note that:
  • Under CKL rules, fixture changes are only permitted under exceptional circumstances and so you will need to provide a strong reason for the change.
  • CKL rules explicitly rule out 'inability to field a full strength team'.
  • Fixtures Officer approval is always required, even if both teams agree.
  • Refereeing clubs don't need to agree to a fixture change as refereeing commitments can be swapped.
Once you have filled in the form and clicked submit, the following will be sent emails:
  • Results and Fixtures Officers.
  • Representative of home and away clubs.
  • Representative of refereeing club.
